Northern Light Innovation

At Northern Light we specialise in the front end of innovation

We believe that connecting disciplines such as business strategy, consumer and brand insight, product development, smart IP strategy and manufacturing/ end-to-end supply chain thinking early in the innovation cycle is what it really takes to deliver meaningful innovation that has strategic impact.

We build value for clients by connecting the power of inventive creativity with world-class expertise across these disciplines, to drive innovation that will win in the marketplace and build a sustainable advantage.

Our philosophy is to help you be more confident and ambitious in your use of technology, consumer and commercial levers to bring breakthrough products and services to market. And to help your company grow for next time through developing capability as we deliver.

If it matters… Invent it
If it matters to your company… Own it.
Put simply, making your inventiveness count.
